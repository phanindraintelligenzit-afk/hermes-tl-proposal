Hermes for
Every Team Lead

Give every team lead at IntelligenzIT their own AI agent. It learns their team's workflows through conversation, automates the repetitive stuff, and helps the whole team move faster.

📅 July 2026 👤 Phani → PVR 🏢 IntelligenzIT

The Problem

Team leads are our most expensive bottleneck — and they're buried in operational overhead.

📋

Attendance

Every morning: who's in, who's late, who's on leave. Every evening: who logged what. 2-3 hours a week chasing this.

📝

End-of-Day Reports

Each team member writes one. TL compiles, formats, sends upward. 3-4 hours a week on copy-paste work.

🗣️

Standups

Reading 5-10 updates, summarizing for management, chasing missing entries. 2-3 hours a week.

🔍

Ad-Hoc Queries

"What's the status on X?" "Who's working on Y?" "Is the blocker resolved?" 3-5 hours a week of context-switching.

~15 hours a week per team lead spent on things that don't require their expertise. That's 40% of their week. For 5 team leads, that's 75 hours a week of lost engineering and strategy capacity.

The Vision

Not a tool they learn. An assistant that learns them.

💬

Onboarding = A Conversation

The agent doesn't come pre-loaded with anything. It asks: "What does your team do? Who are the members? What does a typical day look like? What tasks do you repeat every week?"

The team lead answers in plain English. The agent saves everything. No training data. No configuration files. No coding.

🧠

It Remembers Everything

Every team member's name, role, and preferences. Every recurring task. Every SOP. Every decision. It learns across sessions, builds skills from experience, and gets better over time.

When a team lead leaves, their Hermes profile transfers to the new person. Zero knowledge loss.

"We're already running Hermes. This is just giving every team lead their own copy — with their team's context, their workflows, their automations."

How It Works

No new infrastructure. No new tools. Just Hermes profiles.

STEP 1

Create a Profile

We create a new Hermes profile for each team lead. A profile is just a directory with isolated memory, skills, and config. Takes 5 minutes.

STEP 2

Onboarding Conversation

The team lead talks to their Hermes for 30 minutes. Answers questions about their team, workflows, pain points. The agent learns and saves everything.

STEP 3

Set Up Automations

Hermes proposes automations based on what it learned: "I can set up attendance tracking. Want me to?" TL approves. Cron jobs run from day one.

STEP 4

Connect to Slack

Hermes joins the team's Slack channel. Team members can message it directly. The TL gets reports delivered without lifting a finger.

Running on: Your laptop — no VPS needed. Connected to: Slack and Telegram — already set up. For 5 team leads: just 5 profiles on the same machine.

Security & Control

Self-hosted, open source, auditable. No third-party data leaks.

User Allowlists

Only approved team members can interact with their TL's Hermes.

Command Approval

Destructive actions are flagged and reviewed before execution.

Secret Redaction

API keys, passwords, tokens are auto-masked in all output.

Prompt Injection Protection

Malicious instructions in files or web pages are blocked.

Profile Isolation

Each team lead's data is fully separated from every other.

PII Redaction

Phone numbers, emails can be stripped from agent context.

Self-Hosted

All data stays on your machine. OpenRouter sees only anonymized prompts. No training on your data.

Open Source

MIT-licensed. Fully auditable. No vendor lock-in. We can inspect every line of code.
